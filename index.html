Welcome to Inside the Lines!

"A Trading Cards Interactive and Immersive Experience"


“Inside the lines” is a service that allows the user to see through the lens of their favorite players, coaches, and in the stands all from the comfort of their home or the stadium. The user will purchase a digital trading card pack to unlock access to the subscription. Whether you are at home or in the stands, you will now be able to experience the game first hand using “Inside the Lines”. As technology is at the forefront of the 21st century, we are now capable of having real life experiences through multiple perspectives. Inside the lines has the ability to break the huddle, line up and briefly experience an aspect of the game from the players perspective. In years past, the NFL game has not incorporated virtual reality. This would be an opportunity to break the market with a new engaging platform. “Inside the Lines” comes with two levels; “At Home” and “Stadium Plus+”. Within these levels comes packages that enhance the overall experience of a football game. At Home includes a Fan Package, Coaches Package, and a Players Package. Stadium Plus+ consists of Automated View, Coaches Package, and Players Package. It will also include an added feature to both levels that share NFL facts and live statistics throughout the game. As we further develop and strengthen this product, we desire to amplify our customers interactions and satisfaction with the game.
